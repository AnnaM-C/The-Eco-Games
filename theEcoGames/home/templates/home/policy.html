GDPR ACTS.

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a regulation of the European Union (EU) that sets out rules for the protection of the personal data of EU citizens. The GDPR became enforceable on May 25, 2018, and has been incorporated into UK law through the Data Protection Act 2018.

The GDPR also gives individuals the right to access, modify, and delete their personal data held by organizations. Organizations must provide individuals with clear and transparent information about the processing of their data and must report any data breaches to the relevant authorities within 72 hours.
